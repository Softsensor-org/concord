# SEO Monitoring Review

## Required Inputs

- Prior batch.
- Latest registered source state.
- Monitoring windows.
- Request queue.

## Required Output

- Closed findings with closure reason.
- Reopened findings with reason.
- Deferred findings with next check date.
- New follow-up tickets when tool, owner, or risk differs.

## Required Labels

- `indexed`
- `closed-expected-excluded`
- `still-not-indexed`
- `directional-only`
- `not-comparable`
