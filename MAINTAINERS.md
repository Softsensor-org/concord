# Maintainers

This file lists the current maintainers of Concord — the people with merge
rights who review pull requests, triage issues, and shepherd releases. How
maintainers are added is described in [GOVERNANCE.md](./GOVERNANCE.md).

## Current maintainers

| Maintainer | Affiliation | Contact | Area |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Vivek Gupta (lead) | Softsensor | vivek.gupta@softsensor.ai | Core, governance engine, releases |
| `@softsensor/concord-maintainers` | Softsensor | — | Core, governance engine, releases |

> The maintainer team is currently staffed by Softsensor. As the community grows,
> additional maintainers will be listed here. For most things, reach maintainers
> through GitHub issues and pull requests; for security and conduct matters use
> the private channels in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) and
> [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).

## Contacting maintainers

- **General questions, bugs, features:** open a GitHub issue.
- **Pull request review:** request review on your PR; a maintainer will pick it
  up (best-effort, typically within a few business days).
- **Security reports:** follow [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) — do not open a
  public issue.
- **Code of Conduct reports:** see [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
